About us

Our vison

To be a leading center for agricultural and market information to farmers in yumbe district for maximum resource utilization and improved livelihood.

Our mission

To provide relevant, timely, agricultural information and market linkages to farmers through community involvement for maximum resource utilization and improved livelihood.

Our objectives

    To link buyers of agricultural produce to the farmers in bidibidi refugee settlement.
    To provide agricultural and advisory services to farmers in the settlement.
    To link farmers to agro input sellers in the area e.g. seed companies.
